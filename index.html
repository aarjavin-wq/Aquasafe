About AquaSafe
BIS IS:10500:2012 — Classification Ranges
Safe pH6.5 – 8.5
pH marginal bandNone — outside is Unsafe
Safe TDS< 500 ppm
Marginal TDS500 – 2000 ppm
Safe turbidity< 1 NTU
Marginal turbidity1 – 5 NTU
Classification Logic
Water is SAFE only when pH is 6.5–8.5, TDS < 500 ppm, and turbidity < 1 NTU.

Water is MARGINAL when pH is within range but TDS is 500–2000 ppm or turbidity is 1–5 NTU. Boiling recommended.

Water is UNSAFE if pH is outside 6.5–8.5 (no marginal band per IS:10500:2012), TDS ≥ 2000 ppm, or turbidity ≥ 5 NTU.
Scientific Principles
pH Sensor
E = E₀ − 0.0592 × pH
Nernst equation — electrode potential at 25°C
TDS Sensor
conductivity ∝ ion concentration
Kohlrausch's Law of Independent Ion Migration
Turbidity Sensor
I = I₀ × e^(−μd)
Beer–Lambert Law — light attenuation through medium
Temperature Correction (TDS)
TDS_c = TDS_raw ÷ (1 + 0.02 × (T − 25))
Applied automatically — conductivity rises ~2% per °C

About
AquaSafe was built for URJA 2026 under the theme Health & Wellness. An ESP32 microcontroller reads four sensors and transmits data via Bluetooth LE to this app. Classification follows BIS IS:10500:2012, the Bureau of Indian Standards Indian Drinking Water Specification.
